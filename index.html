About Me

I am an enthusiastic and dedicated software developer passionate about automating and simplifying complex processes through integrated software solutions. I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Development with a concentration in Software Design & Programming from DeVry University. Having already obtained my Associate of Applied Science in Information Technology & Networking and keeping my GPA at a perfect 4.00, I am committed to both academic achievement and professional growth.

With hands-on experience in various programming languages, including HTML, CSS, Java, JavaScript, and MySQL, as well as a solid foundation in Python, I excel at developing robust software solutions. My proficiency extends to working with big data tools and frameworks.
